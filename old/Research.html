Mining Evidences for

Entity Disambiguation

LEARN MORE

This project aims at mining disambiguation evidences from external corpus to bridge the information gap between queries and the reference knowledge base. We propose a generative model and an incremental algorithm to automatically mine useful evidences across documents. With a specific modeling of "background topic" and "unknown entities", our model is able to harvest useful evidences out of noisy information. Experimental results show that our proposed method outperforms the state-of-the-art approaches significantly.

Minimum Substring

Partitioning

LEARN MORE

This project aims to reduce the huge memory footprint in de novo assembly of large scale DNA sequences. We proposed the Minimum Substring Partitioning technique and sucessfully applied it to efficiently finishing the k-mer counting and de Bruijn graph construction tasks in DNA sequence assembly with very small memory footprint.
